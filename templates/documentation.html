Below is an explanation of how we cleaned our unique data so that we could universally compare each county with one another:

Because we are pulling county data from multiple different websites and there is no website that contains all the counties' yearly expenditure data, we-the coders- had to universalize the dataset in order to compare county data. The reason for this is because not every county had identical department names in their expenditure summary. For example, Loudoun county expenditure summary may define library expenses simply as Library, whereas Prince William's expenditure summary may define library expenditure as Library and Staff. The way in which we were able to universalize the dataset so that we could compare similar departments was to group similar departments into 7 “bins” which we defined and created. The 7 bins are: Governing(Governance), Social Services, Education, Public Safety, Community Development, Finances, and Other.

1. Examples of departments we defined as Governing: County Council, Public Affairs, County Administrators and Board Supervisors, Commissioners, Judicial Courts and more.

2. Examples of departments we defined as Social Services: Human Services, Family Services, Health, Adult Centers/Old Age Homes, Technology, General Information, Mapping and Geographic information, and more.

3. Examples of departments we defined as Education: Schools (Colleges, Universities, Middle and High Schools), Libraries and more.

4. Examples of departments we defined as Public Safety: Emergency Management, Sheriff, Fire and Rescue, Police, Chemical Safety Board (CSB), Fire and Rescue, and more.

5. Examples of departments we defined as Community Development: Building Development, Reconstruction, Planning and Zoning, Transportation, Vehicle Services, Economic Development and more.

6. Examples of departments we defined as Finances: Capital Funds, Non-Profits, Insurances, Treasurers, Finance and Procurement, Management and Budget, Employee Benefits, and more.

7. Examples of departments we defined as Other: Non-Departmental, General Services, Animal Services, Cable & Consumer Services and more.
